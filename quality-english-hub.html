// 02 — common sentence patterns to decode

義務・要求の読み方
The supplier shall submit a corrective action plan within 10 business days of notification.
サプライヤーは通知から10営業日以内に是正処置計画を提出しなければならない。
→ 「shall」は義務。期限に注意して読む。
CSRの上乗せ要求パターン
In addition to the requirements of IATF 16949 clause 7.1.5.1, the supplier shall also register calibration records in the SQP portal.
IATF 16949 条項7.1.5.1の要求に加えて、サプライヤーはSQPポータルへの校正記録登録も行わなければならない。
→ 「In addition to」= 上乗せ要求のサイン。元の条項は生きている。
FMEA所見の読み方
The current controls are insufficient to detect the failure mode prior to reaching the customer. Detection rating should be reviewed.
現在の管理方法では、顧客に到達する前に故障モードを検出するには不十分である。検出度評価の見直しが必要。
→ 「insufficient」「should be reviewed」→ 指摘・改善要求のサイン。
監査所見（NC）の読み方
Objective evidence shows that calibration records for gauge #A-012 were not available during the audit. This is a nonconformity against IATF 16949 clause 7.1.5.1.
客観的証拠として、ゲージ#A-012の校正記録が監査中に入手できなかった。これはIATF 16949 条項7.1.5.1に対する不適合である。
→ 「Objective evidence shows that...」= 証拠ベースの指摘パターン。

// 03 — fill-in templates for common documents

8D Corrective Action Report — 全体構成
D1 — Team
Champion: [Manager name / 部門] Team Leader: [Your name / 役職] Team Members: [QA, Production, Engineering — 各担当者名]
D2 — Problem Description
What: [不具合の内容 — 具体的に] Where: Found at [発見場所 — customer line / incoming inspection / etc.] When: First reported on [日付] How many: [不良数 / 影響ロット数] Impact: [顧客ライン停止の有無、クレーム番号等]
※ 5W1Hで書く。数字を必ず入れる。
D3 — Containment Actions (24hr)
1. Isolated and quarantined all suspect parts at [場所] on [日時]. 2. Sorted [数量] parts at customer's facility. Result: [OK数 / NG数]. 3. Confirmed no further nonconforming parts in transit. Effectiveness verified by: [確認者・手段]
D4 — Root Cause Analysis
Occurrence cause (Why did it happen?): Why 1: [現象] Why 2: [なぜ1の原因] Why 3: [なぜ2の原因] Why 4: [なぜ3の原因] Root cause: [根本原因] Escape cause (Why was it not detected?): Why 1: [流出した理由] Root cause: [流出根本原因]
※ 発生原因と流出原因は必ず分けて書く。
D5 — Chosen Corrective Actions
For occurrence: [恒久対策の内容] For escape: [流出防止対策の内容] Verification method: [どう効果確認するか] Target completion: [日付]
D6 — Implementation
Action 1: [対策内容] — Owner: [担当者] — Completed: [日付] Action 2: [対策内容] — Owner: [担当者] — Completed: [日付] Documents updated: Control Plan Rev.[番号], FMEA Rev.[番号]
D7 — Preventive Actions (Lessons Learned)
Similar processes / products reviewed: [水平展開先] Actions taken: [内容] QMS documents updated: [更新した文書一覧]
D8 — Team Recognition
This 8D report is approved and closed on [日付]. Approved by: [品質管理責任者名・署名] All corrective actions have been verified as effective.
4M Change Notification — 変更通知書
Header
Change Notification No.: [CN-2024-XXX] Supplier: [自社名] Customer: [顧客名] Affected Part No.: [品番] Submission Date: [日付] Requested Implementation Date: [希望実施日]
Change Description
Category of change: [Machine / Method / Material / Man] Description of change: Current: [現状の内容] Proposed: [変更後の内容] Reason for change: [理由 — 老朽化、タクト改善、コスト等]
Impact Assessment
Impact on product quality characteristics: [None / Yes — describe] Impact on Control Plan parameters: [None / Yes — CP will be revised] Impact on Special Characteristics (SC): [None / Yes — describe] Validation plan: [試生産数量・検査内容・判定基準]
Approval Request
We kindly request your approval to proceed with the above change. Please confirm your decision by [回答期限]. Contact: [担当者名 / メール / 電話]
Audit Finding — Nonconformity (NC)
Finding Header
Finding No.: [NC-01] Clause Reference: IATF 16949:[条項番号 e.g. 7.1.5.1] / CSR Clause [番号] Classification: [Minor / Major] Nonconformity
Statement of Nonconformity
Objective evidence shows that [具体的な事実を記述]. This does not conform to the requirement of [条項番号], which states: "[該当する条項の要求内容を引用]"
※ 必ず「Objective evidence shows that」で始め、事実のみを記述する。意見・推測を入れない。
Evidence Collected
- [記録名・文書番号 e.g. Calibration record for gauge #A-012] - Interview with [役職・氏名] on [日付] - [写真・サンプル等]
Required Action
The auditee is required to submit a corrective action plan addressing: 1. Root cause of the nonconformity 2. Corrective action to eliminate the root cause 3. Evidence of implementation Due date: [日付 — Minorは30日、Majorは即時〜14日]
Audit Finding — Opportunity for Improvement (OFI)
OFI Statement
Finding No.: [OFI-01] Area: [該当プロセス・部門] During the audit, it was observed that [現状の観察事実]. While this does not constitute a nonconformity, the audit team suggests that [改善の方向性] could further strengthen the effectiveness of the quality management system.
※ OFIは義務ではない。「suggest / consider / may wish to」などの表現を使う。「shall」は使わない。
Potential Benefit
Implementing this improvement could result in: - [期待される効果1] - [期待される効果2]
メール定型文 — 状況別5パターン
① 不具合発生の第一報（24時間以内）
Subject: [URGENT] Quality Issue Notification — Part No. [品番] Dear [SQE名], We are writing to inform you of a quality issue identified at [発見場所] on [日付]. Issue summary: [不具合内容を1〜2文で] Quantity affected: [数量] Containment actions have been initiated: - [封じ込め処置の内容] A full 8D report will be submitted by [日付]. Please let us know if you require any immediate action. Best regards, [氏名 / 役職 / 連絡先]
② 変更通知の事前確認
Subject: 4M Change Notification — Prior Approval Request — [品番] Dear [SQE名], We would like to notify you of a planned change and request your prior approval before implementation. Change summary: [変更内容を1〜2文で] Planned implementation date: [日付] Please find the attached Change Notification Form (CN-[番号]) for your review. We kindly request your approval by [回答期限]. Best regards, [氏名 / 役職]
③ CSRの解釈確認（評価済みの上での照会）
Subject: Clarification Request — CSR Clause [番号] — [品番/案件名] Dear [SQE名], We have reviewed the Customer Specific Requirements (Rev.[版数]) regarding clause [番号]. Based on our internal assessment, we have concluded that [社内評価の結論]. However, as this is a first occurrence of this situation, we would appreciate your confirmation of our interpretation. Specifically, our question is: [具体的な質問を1文で] Thank you for your guidance. Best regards, [氏名 / 役職]
④ 監査日程調整
Subject: Supplier Audit Scheduling — [会社名] — [年月] Dear [担当者名], We would like to schedule a quality system audit at your facility. Proposed date(s): [第一候補 / 第二候補] Scope: [監査範囲 — IATF 16949 / specific processes] Duration: Approximately [時間数] Please confirm your availability or suggest alternative dates. We will share the audit plan at least [X] days in advance. Best regards, [氏名 / 役職 / 会社名]
⑤ 是正処置の進捗フォローアップ
Subject: Follow-up — Corrective Action Status — 8D No. [番号] Dear [担当者名], This is a follow-up regarding the corrective action submitted for the issue reported on [日付]. According to our records, the following actions are due by [日付]: - [アクション内容] Could you please provide a status update at your earliest convenience? Best regards, [氏名 / 役職]
